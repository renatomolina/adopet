WANT TO ADOPT OR LOST YOUR PET?

Adopet is an app that can help you find pets on the street, or temporary homes. The app provides you a new way to find pets based on location and pet characteristics, like color, breed and pet size, which make the search easier and faster!



HOW IT WORKS?

Basically you can use Adopet anytime you see a lost animal. A dog, cat, bird or any other animal that someone might by looking for, or that needs a new home. The animal could be lost on the street or in a temporary home for adoption. Of if you lost your pet, or want to adopet you can search pets by location or characteristics.

SEARCH

You can use characteristics of your pet to help the search when you lost it. E.g: breed, fur, color. Or if you want to adopet, but have a small apartment you can search by size too.

PHOTOS

To help you find your pet you can see pictures that were taken for each pet. This will help guarantee that the pet registered is yours.

LOCATION

If your lost your pet near a park, you can see which pets were found near that park on the map. Or near your home, or current location. Adopet provides a map with pinpoints indicading each pet and a list. Temporary homes, or adoption points can be found with lots of animals listed on the same spot.

What adopet does

Adopet features help people adopt a new pet, find their pets and provides a tool so that society can collaborate and help animals find new homes.

Find a pet for you

Do you know that in most countries there are more pets in the street than in homes? Adopet can help you find a pet as you always wanted. Why buy a pet if there are so many expecting you, and needing care on the streets?

Find with map help

Adopet search uses geolocation range in a map to help you find your lost pet.
