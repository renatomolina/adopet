LOST YOUR PET OR WANT TO ADOPT ?

Adopet is an app that can help you find pets on the street, or temporary homes. The app provides you a new way to find pets based on location and pet characteristics, like color, breed and pet size, which make the search easier and faster!

What is adopet?

Basically you can use Adopet anytime you see a lost animal. A dog, cat, bird or any other animal that someone might by looking for, or that needs a new home.

The animal could be lost on the street or in a temporary home for adoption. Of if you lost your pet, or want to adopet you can search pets by location or characteristics.

How it works?

You can use characteristics of your pet to help the search when you lost it. E.g: breed, fur, color. Or if you want to adopet, but have a small apartment you can search by size too.

To help you find your pet you can see pictures that were taken for each pet. This will help guarantee that the pet registered is yours.

If your lost your pet near a park, home or current location, you can see which pets were found near that park on the map. Adopet provides a map with pinpoints indicading each pet and a list. Temporary homes, or adoption points can be found with lots of animals listed on the same spot.
